The SR-25: Ultimate Tactical Precision.

The SR-25 series of ri­fles is the end result of decades of improvement and refinement to the AR-10 type ri­fle platform.
While each model is oriented to a specific precision role, they all share the same high reliability, long critical part life, and intuitive ambidextrous controls.
The SR-25 has been tested and validated by the harshest combat conditions; proving time and again to perform as required.
Barrel and bolt life are significantly increased, reducing the need for part replacement and extending maintenance cycles.
