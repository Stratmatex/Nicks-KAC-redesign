The FIRE-LR: Long Range Thermal Sight.

The FIRE-LR is a compact lightweight high resolution uncooled long-wave infrared clip-on inline weapon sight. It was developed to provide long range target detection and recognition without altering the day scope’s point of impact.

A 10μm sensor allows for detection of humans at 2,150 meters in clean air, and 1,651 meters in obscured battlefield conditions. The FIRE-LR has a 4.5 hour operation span, powered by three CR123A batteries.

At 1.6 pounds and 7.6 inches in length, it is a class-leader in capability and warfighter load. The FIRE-LR can also be used in hand held mode to facilitate long range observation, with or without the removable magnifier.
